The power of ML

We need to think about
renewable energy

Solar cells are indispensable in our quest for sustainable energy solutions.
They signify a revolutionary technology that taps into the boundless energy of the sun
and transforms it into electricity. Unlike conventional energy sources such as fossil fuels,
solar cells generate clean energy without releasing detrimental greenhouse gases or pollutants into the atmosphere.

We decided to make a machine learning algorithm that could predict Solar Cells accurately. The code for the ML model is available on GitHub. It can predict with a fair accuracy perovskite materials that can be stably used to construct solar cells. It is deployed on render. Click here to go there.
Note: As it is deployed on a serverless instance, it shuts down with inactivity, refresh a few times the webpage to make it work.
